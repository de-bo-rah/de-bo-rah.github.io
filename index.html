hi, i'm debarupa
this is where i dump my eclecticism as an attempt to protrude the transcendental ( in short : my space on the web )

ever since i have known myself, or ever since I have begun to get attuned with what i like and what i don't -- i realised i write as much as i read, both of which belongs to the same bracket of my life, the one which seeks contentment and also recieves every part of it. and with every instance there's five hundred different ideas in my head punctuated with a genuine desire to learn something new. so that's what i am and my indentity ( if there's one at all metaphysically ) revolves around : the quest of knowledge, to become a life long learner. and that's what i do.

i like reading old ( and new ) and latest research papers about neural networks, i also like to train them sometimes. i like mathematics and this likeness alone helped me fetch a few recognitions though i realised the kind of math ( pre college ) we do in our country is more of a exam clearing strategy and less of an academic pursuit. i like essay films and i like to write about them. and i like to read a lot. i had a writing-poems stint briefly because i wanted to. and i also develop special interests in various subjects just because i want to. anyway i guess i will be shifting my write ups here because medium dot com has started behaving really dicey at least in my device. that's all... end of transmission.
